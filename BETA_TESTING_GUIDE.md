# FiftyAF Minute Moment - Beta Testing Guide

Welcome to the beta testing program for **FiftyAF Minute Moment**, a daily reflection card app designed specifically for Gen X users seeking mindful personal growth. This guide will help you install the app, understand what to test, and provide valuable feedback.

---

## What is FiftyAF Minute Moment?

FiftyAF Minute Moment is a mobile app that delivers daily reflection cards with audio insights to help you build self-awareness, process emotions, and maintain mental clarity. Each card features beautiful geometric designs and a 77-second audio insight ending with the signature phrase "Be Kind & Curious."

**Key Features:**
- **Daily Card Draw**: Automatically draws a new card each day
- **Manual Shuffle**: Draw additional cards whenever you need guidance
- **Audio Insights**: Calming male narration with practical wisdom
- **Reflection Journal**: Write and save your thoughts
- **Streak Tracking**: Monitor your consistency with milestone celebrations
- **Favorites**: Save cards that resonate with you
- **21 Cards**: Organized across 7 life domains (Self, Relationships, Purpose, Vitality, Creativity, Growth, Wisdom)

## How to Install the Beta

The beta version runs through **Expo Go**, a free app that lets you test mobile apps before they're published to the App Store.

### Step 1: Install Expo Go

**On iPhone:**
1. Open the App Store
2. Search for "Expo Go"
3. Install the free Expo Go app

**On Android:**
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "Expo Go"
3. Install the free Expo Go app

### Step 2: Access the Beta App

You'll receive a **QR code** or **direct link** from the developer. 

**Option A: QR Code (Recommended)**
1. Open your phone's camera app
2. Point it at the QR code
3. Tap the notification that appears
4. The app will open in Expo Go

**Option B: Direct Link**
1. Open the link on your phone
2. Select "Open in Expo Go"
3. The app will load automatically

### Step 3: Complete Onboarding

The first time you open the app, you'll see a 4-page onboarding experience explaining:
- The purpose of the app
- Journaling tips
- How to build a daily habit
- How to navigate the app

Swipe through all four pages and tap "Get Started" to begin your 3-day free trial.

## What to Test

As a beta tester, your feedback is invaluable. Please test all major features and note any issues or suggestions.

### Core Features to Test

**1. Daily Card Draw**
- Open the app each day to see if a new card is automatically drawn
- Check if the card image displays correctly
- Verify the card title and domain are visible
- Test the audio player by tapping "Play Insight"
- Confirm the audio plays smoothly and ends with "Be Kind & Curious"

**2. Manual Card Draw**
- Tap "Draw New Card" on the Today screen
- Verify a different card appears
- Test multiple draws to see card variety

**3. Shuffle Feature**
- Navigate to the "Shuffle" tab
- Tap "Shuffle Deck" and watch the animation
- Tap "Select This Card" to view the drawn card
- Confirm it navigates to the card detail screen

**4. Card Library**
- Navigate to the "Library" tab
- Browse all 21 cards in the grid view
- Test the domain filters (All, Self, Relationships, Purpose, etc.)
- Tap on any card to view its detail page

**5. Favorites**
- On any card screen, tap the heart button to favorite it
- Verify the heart fills in and shows "Favorited"
- Go to the Library tab and select the "Favorites" filter
- Confirm your favorited cards appear
- Unfavorite a card and verify it's removed from the Favorites filter

**6. Reflection Journal**
- Navigate to the "Journal" tab
- Tap the "+" button to create a new entry
- Write a reflection about today's card
- Tap "Save" and verify the entry appears in your journal list
- Tap on an entry to view or edit it
- Test deleting an entry

**7. Streak Tracking**
- Open the app on consecutive days
- Check if the streak counter on the home screen increases
- Look for milestone alerts (3, 7, 21, 30, 66 days)

**8. Notifications**
- Go to Settings tab
- Enable "Daily Reminders"
- Grant notification permission when prompted
- Tap "Change Time" to set a custom reminder time
- Scroll through hours (0-23) and minutes (0, 15, 30, 45)
- Save your preferred time
- Wait for the notification to arrive at the scheduled time

**9. Social Sharing**
- On any card screen, tap "Share"
- Verify the share sheet opens with card details
- Test sharing to a messaging app or social media

**10. Settings & Subscription**
- Navigate to Settings
- Check your trial status (should show "X days remaining")
- Tap "Subscribe Now" to view the paywall
- Note: Do not complete payment during beta unless instructed

## What to Look For

### Bugs to Report

- **Crashes**: Does the app close unexpectedly?
- **Freezes**: Does the app stop responding?
- **Visual glitches**: Are images, text, or buttons misaligned or cut off?
- **Audio issues**: Does the audio fail to play, skip, or sound distorted?
- **Navigation problems**: Do buttons not work or navigate to the wrong screen?
- **Data loss**: Do journal entries, favorites, or streaks disappear?
- **Notification failures**: Do reminders not arrive at the scheduled time?

### User Experience Feedback

- **Onboarding**: Is it clear what the app does and how to use it?
- **Navigation**: Can you easily find all features?
- **Design**: Does the berry color theme feel appropriate? Are cards visually appealing?
- **Audio**: Is the voice calming? Is the insight length (77 seconds) appropriate?
- **Journaling**: Is the journal interface intuitive? Do you want more prompts?
- **Streaks**: Does the streak feature motivate you?
- **Performance**: Does the app feel fast and responsive?

### Content Feedback

- **Card Variety**: Do you feel like you're seeing the same cards too often?
- **Card Relevance**: Do the cards resonate with you as a Gen X user?
- **Audio Insights**: Are the insights helpful and thought-provoking?
- **Journaling Tips**: Are the tips in the onboarding and journal screens useful?

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to pay during the beta?**  
A: No. The 3-day trial and subscription system are built into the app, but you should not complete payment during beta testing unless specifically instructed by the developer.

**Q: Will my data be saved when the app launches publicly?**  
A: Beta data (journal entries, favorites, streaks) is stored locally on your device. When the app launches publicly, you'll start fresh unless the developer provides a migration path.

**Q: Can I share the beta with friends?**  
A: Please check with the developer first. Beta access may be limited to a specific group.

**Q: What if I can't install Expo Go due to an Apple account issue?**  
A: Contact the developer. They may be able to provide an alternative installation method (TestFlight) or wait until your account issue is resolved.

**Q: How often should I test the app?**  
A: Ideally, open the app daily to test the streak tracking and daily card draw features. Test other features (journal, shuffle, favorites) at least 2-3 times per week.

**Q: What happens after beta testing ends?**  
A: The app will be submitted to the App Store and Google Play Store. You'll be notified when the public version is available for download.
